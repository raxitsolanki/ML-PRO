Health Suggestions

Personalized lifestyle adjustments and medical insights to keep your health on track.

Dietary Guide

Stable blood sugar begins with the right plate. Focus on low-GI Indian staples.

  • Ragi & Multigrain staples
  • Moong Dal & Legumes
  • Leafy Greens & Salads
  • Low-GI Fruits (Guava)

Warning Signs

Recognizing early signals helps prevent serious long-term complications.

  • Thirst & Frequent Urination
  • Slow Wound Healing
  • Persistent Fatigue
  • Numbness in Hands/Feet

Daily Routine

Consistency in your schedule improves natural insulin sensitivity.

  • Fixed Meal Timings
  • Regular Glucose Checks
  • Post-Meal Active Walks
  • No-Skip Breakfast Rule

Monitoring

Proactive tracking screenings help detect complications early.

  • HbA1c (Every 3 Months)
  • Blood Sugar Logs
  • Eye & Foot Exams
  • Kidney/Lipid (Yearly)

Target Levels

Recommended ranges for long-term health and metabolic control.

  • Fasting: 80–130 mg/dL
  • Post-Meal: < 180 mg/dL
  • HbA1c Goal: Below 7%
  • BP: Below 140/90 mmHg

Safety Protocol

Essential protocols to prevent dangerous blood sugar fluctuations.

  • Consistent Daily Timings
  • Proper Insulin Storage
  • Hypoglycemia Awareness
  • No Sudden Dose Changes

Healthy Eating

Focus on a nutrient-rich diet to maintain energy and long-term metabolic health.

  • Whole Grains & Millets
  • High Fiber Green Salads
  • Low-fat Dairy & Proteins
  • Portion Control Habits

Physical Activity

Regular movement is the most effective way to prevent future chronic conditions.

  • 150 min Brisk Walking / Week
  • Muscle Strengthening (2 days)
  • Flexibility & Yoga Exercises
  • Aim for 8k - 10k Steps daily

Do’s & Don’ts

Small daily adjustments to your lifestyle can lead to massive health improvements.

  • Avoid Late-night Heavy Meals
  • Limit Refined Flour & Sugars
  • Track Body Weight Regularly
  • Drink 3-4 Liters of Water

Risk Reduction

Understand how to keep chronic metabolic conditions at bay effectively.

  • Maintain Healthy Waist Size
  • Quit Tobacco & Smoking
  • Annual Lipid Profile Tests
  • Early Detection Screenings

Prediabetes Risk

Know the signs and risk factors to reverse potential diabetes early.

  • Family History Screening
  • BMI Management (Target 23)
  • Blood Pressure Monitoring
  • Sedentary Habit Awareness

Mental Wellness

Cortisol (stress hormone) management is key to metabolic balance.

  • Deep Breathing & Mindfulness
  • Screen-free Sleeping Habits
  • Quality Social Engagement
  • Work-Life Harmony Goals
